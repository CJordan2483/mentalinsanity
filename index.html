Welcome to Mental Insanity

A multimedia IP born from the darkest depths of the mind of CJ Jordan, guitarist, survivor, builder of worlds.

Shaped by a lifetime of mental health struggles, creative isolation, and the need to turn chaos into meaning, this project traces its roots back to childhood nights spent devouring horror stories, Goosebumps paperbacks, and Scary Stories to Tell in the Dark.

What started as short stories, riffs, and sketches became a way to survive. A method of translating pain, fear, and resilience into sound, worlds, and characters.

Mental Insanity stands as both a creative platform and a personal record. Built by a suicide survivor who chose to keep creating instead of disappearing.

Mental Insanity Records is the first division rising from the void.
